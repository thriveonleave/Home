Our Story

You went into survival mode.
This is your way back.

The name says everything: Thrive on Leave. Not survive it. Not get through it. Thrive — right in the middle of it.

Because here's what nobody tells you before the baby arrives: maternity leave is one of the most disorienting seasons a woman will ever walk through. You love this tiny person with everything you have. And at the same time, you're exhausted in a way that has no language. Your identity quietly shifts. The city keeps moving. And somewhere between the night feeds and the quiet afternoons, survival mode sets in — and it starts to feel like the only mode available.

Most mothers know this feeling intimately. The slow disappearing. The way you stop making plans for yourself. The way Los Angeles — this extraordinary, alive, endlessly interesting city — starts to feel like it belongs to someone else now.

"Thrive on Leave is the gentle reminder that you are still here — and that the city is still yours."

— Thrive on Leave

This newsletter was built as an invitation — not a demand. There's no pressure to do it all or show up perfectly. It's a soft nudge, delivered every week, to take one intentional moment for yourself. To remember what lights you up. To step back into community, slowly and on your own terms.

Every issue is curated with that in mind: experiences across Los Angeles that are worthy of your time, your energy, and the small windows you have. Free mornings and high-ticket evenings. Solo outings and spaces to gather with other women who understand exactly where you are.

You don't have to leave survival mode all at once. You just have to take one small step back toward yourself. We'll be here every Wednesday, with somewhere beautiful to go.
